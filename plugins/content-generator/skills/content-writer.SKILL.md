# Content Generator Skill

## Best Practices

### Before Content Generation
1. Clearly define target audience
2. Specify business objectives
3. Provide brand guidelines
4. Include key messages
5. Mention any constraints (length, format, tone)

### Optimization Tips
1. **Headlines**: Use numbers, questions, or emotional triggers
2. **CTA**: Make clear, action-oriented calls-to-action
3. **Structure**: Use subheadings and bullet points for readability
4. **SEO**: Include relevant keywords naturally
5. **Mobile**: Keep social content concise and scannable

## Advanced Features

### Multi-Channel Campaigns
Generate coordinated content for multiple platforms simultaneously with consistent messaging but channel-optimized formatting.

### Brand Voice Customization
